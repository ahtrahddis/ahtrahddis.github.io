Who Am I?

I’m a secular Buddhist and an amateur photographer.

I used to be a pragmatic Mahasi noting geek, obsessed with the Progress of Insight map.

My current practice is heavily influenced by Asin Tejaniya, various secular non-dual teachers (Diana Winston, Loch Kelly, Sam Harris, etc.) and Soto Zen.
